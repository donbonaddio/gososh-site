Frequently asked questions

How do I start the free trial?

Open GoSosh, complete the welcome flow, and tap Start 7-day free trial on the paywall. The trial begins immediately. You won't be charged unless you keep your subscription past day 7.

How do I cancel my subscription?

iOS handles all subscription cancellations. Go to Settings → Apple Account → Subscriptions → GoSosh and tap Cancel. You'll keep access until the end of the current billing period.

Cancelling during the 7-day trial means you'll never be charged.

How do I add a card?

On the Cards tab, tap the + button in the top-right. Pick a starter template or build your own. You can edit the subject, lens, refresh cadence, or add per-source URL overrides before saving.

Why aren't my cards updating?

Cards refresh automatically on their configured cadence (default: daily). To force a refresh now, pull down on the Cards tab.

If a specific source is consistently returning no results, the upstream service may be down or rate-limiting. You can also try disabling that source temporarily in Settings → Sources.

Where do I get a YouTube API key?

YouTube source requires a Data API v3 key from Google Cloud Console. The free quota (10,000 units/day) is plenty for personal use. Step-by-step instructions and the signup link are in the in-app Settings → Sources → YouTube section.

Why don't my cards sync to my other devices?

GoSosh syncs through your private iCloud account. To sync between devices:

  • Both devices must be signed into the same Apple ID
  • iCloud Drive must be enabled in iOS Settings → Apple Account → iCloud
  • GoSosh must be enabled under iCloud apps

Sync usually completes within a few seconds of a change. If it stays stuck, force-quit GoSosh and reopen.

Is my data private?

Yes. GoSosh has no servers, no analytics, no tracking. Your cards live only on your devices and in your private iCloud database — we have no way to see them. See the privacy policy for the full story.

  • A screenshot if useful

I want a feature that doesn't exist yet.

Tell me about it. The product roadmap is shaped largely by user feedback. Common requests for the next release: Reddit source (Rev 1), sentiment scoring (Pro tier), iPad version (Pro tier), pagination for high-volume subjects.

Refunds

Refund requests are handled by Apple directly via your Apple Account. To request:

  • Go to reportaproblem.apple.com
  • Sign in with your Apple ID
  • Find GoSosh in your recent purchases
  • Tap "Report" and choose the appropriate reason

Apple reviews refund requests case-by-case, usually within a few days.
