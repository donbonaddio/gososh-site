The news app that doesn’t read what you read.

GoSosh tells you what’s actually happening across hundreds of sources. The AI that powers it runs entirely on your iPhone. No accounts. No servers watching your taste in stories. No “your reading habits help train our model” fine print buried on page 14.

On-device intelligence

Every AI feature in GoSosh runs on your iPhone using Apple Intelligence. Nothing about what you read leaves your device — not for analytics, not for personalization, not for us, not for anyone. We literally cannot see what you do in the app. That’s not a promise. That’s the architecture.

Signal, not noise

GoSosh recognizes when five different outlets are covering the same story — even when they don’t share a single keyword. It surfaces who’s actually being talked about across sources, not just whoever ranked highest on a search page. The Cross-Source widget tells you what’s been confirmed. The Quiet Movers widget tells you what’s about to break.

No chatbot. No summaries. No hallucinations.

We don’t generate text. We don’t paraphrase articles. We don’t make things up. Every tap sends you to the original publisher in a clean in-app reader. The whole pitch is: more time reading what writers actually wrote, less time talking to a robot about it.

If “AI Powered” makes you skeptical, you’re our target customer.
