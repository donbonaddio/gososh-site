The card model.

Every card is one Subject plus one Lens. Mix and match — track yourself with Mentions, a competitor with Buzz, an industry topic with Top voices.

Subject — what you track

  • You Your own name across the web
  • A person you follow Friend, peer, family member
  • A public figure or brand Celebrities, executives, companies
  • A topic or keyword "Swift 6.4", "indie iOS", whatever you care about

Lens — what you want to know

  • Mentions Who’s talking, newest first
  • Buzz Volume + trend chart
  • Top voices Who’s driving the conversation
  • What’s new Freshest items grouped by day
  • Reactions Sentiment tone (coming soon)
